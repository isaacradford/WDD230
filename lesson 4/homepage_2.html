It's About Community:

March Madness at the Library:

Going all month at the idaho falls public library, check out a book from the the 3rd floor and ask a staff member for a ballot. Each week, we'll count the ballots and advance the winning books to the next round. If you vote for the title that wins, you could iwn a Kindle Fire 7 Tablet!

The Art Museum of Eastern Idaho:

Check out the 'State of the Forest' fabric installation by Suze Woolf. In this exhibit, 30 portraits of burned trees have been digitally transferreed onto fabric and hung from the ceiling and are accompanied by stories written by wildland firefighter Lorena Williams.

Winnie and Mo's Bookstore:

Every Saturday at 10:30 AM, join Winnie and Mo in Downtown, Idaho Falls for story time!

It's Our Anniversary!

The Bank of Idaho has been proudly serving Idahoans now for 40 years this march, and we'd like you to celebrate with us! Join us on March 29th at 4 PM at the Snake River Landing Waterfront building for free food, games and entertainment!
