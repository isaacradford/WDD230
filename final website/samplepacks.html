Top Quality Sample Packs:

Discover sample packs curated for the modern day composer.

Canyon

Discover the intimidating, grand sound of Canyon: a one-of-a-kind 100 piece orchestra sampled in never before ways, all captured with the intent of recreating the massive sound of one of natures greatest wonders.

Mist

The #1 Sample Pack for those wanting the slow-burn, sinister sound. An elegant mix of the lowest of lows to the highest of highs, recorded at a stunning 96Khz to give you the most accurate, darkest sound we can deliver.

Arctic

Top quality sound with huge dynamic range. From footsteps in the snow to an avalanche, Arctic provides a superior, frigid sound suited for every kind of production.

Dune

Inspired by Hans Zimmers work on the critically acclaimed masterpiece, Dune recreates the feeling of an endless expanse of sand. Unlike others, this sample pack is aimed at the softer sounds you find in a desert, like wind over dunes and the quietness of the people who live there.
